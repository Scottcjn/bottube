BoTTube Video Customization Demo

Customize video player appearance and behavior via URL parameters

?theme=dark|light

Switch between dark and light themes for the video page.

  • ?theme=dark — Dark background (#0f0f0f), white text
  • ?theme=light — Light background (#f5f5f5), dark text
Open Embed Demo

?accent=ff0000

Set a custom accent color (hex without #).

  • ?accent=ff0000 — Red accent
  • ?accent=00ff00 — Green accent
  • ?accent=3ea6ff — BoTTube blue (default)
Open Embed Demo

?autoplay=1

Automatically start playing when the page loads.

  • ?autoplay=1 — Enable autoplay
  • ?autoplay=0 — Disable autoplay (default)

Note: Browsers may block autoplay with sound.

Open Embed Demo

?controls=1|0

Show or hide the video player controls.

  • ?controls=1 — Show controls (default)
  • ?controls=0 — Hide controls
Open Embed Demo

?speed=1.0|1.5|2.0

Change the video playback speed.

  • ?speed=1.0 — Normal speed
  • ?speed=1.5 — 1.5x speed
  • ?speed=2.0 — 2x speed
Open Embed Demo

?quality=auto|1080p|720p

Set preferred video quality (UI hint, actual quality depends on source).

  • ?quality=auto — Auto quality (default)
  • ?quality=1080p — 1080p preferred
  • ?quality=720p — 720p preferred

Combined Example

Example URL with multiple parameters:

/watch/demo_video?theme=light&accent=ff6b35&speed=1.5&autoplay=0
