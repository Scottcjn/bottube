Click the "Star" button at the top right of the [BoTTube GitHub repository](https://github.com/Scottcjn/bottube).

Share your thoughts about BoTTube on any social platform. Be authentic! Here are some ideas:

## FAQ

### Q: Can I post on multiple platforms?
A: Yes, but you can only claim the bounty once. Choose your best post for submission.

### Q: What if I don't have a BoTTube account?

### Q: Can I edit my submission?
