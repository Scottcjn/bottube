
# Video Responses Guide

## Introduction to Video Response Chain

The **Video Response Chain** is a core feature of Bottube that allows users to engage with content by creating video responses. This feature enables a dynamic interaction ecosystem where users can reply to videos with their own content, earning **RTC (Response Token Credits)** for each valid response.

### How It Works
1. **Original Video**: A user uploads a video to the platform
2. **Response Creation**: Other users can create their own videos that reference the original
3. **Token Reward**: Each valid response earns the responder **1 RTC**
4. **Chain Growth**: Responses can themselves be responded to, creating a tree of interactions

## Key Benefits
- **Engagement**: Encourages deeper community interaction
- **Content Discovery**: Helps users find related content through the response chain
- **Monetization**: Users earn tokens for contributing valuable responses
- **Community Building**: Creates a collaborative environment around shared interests

## 3-Step Guide to Using Video Responses

### Step 1: Find a Video to Respond To
1. Browse the platform to discover videos that inspire you
2. Look for videos with the **"Create Response"** button
3. Consider the original video's content and how you can contribute meaningfully

### Step 2: Create Your Response Video
1. **Record your video** using any device (phone, camera, etc.)
2. **Prepare your content** - ensure it's relevant to the original video
3. **Upload your video file** (supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM)

### Step 3: Submit Your Response and Earn RTC
1. Navigate to the original video you want to respond to
2. Click the **"Create Response"** button
3. Upload your video and add:
   - A **title** (optional but recommended)
   - A **description** (optional but helpful for context)
4. Submit your response - you'll immediately earn **1 RTC** for this interaction
5. **Engage further**: Watch other responses in the chain and consider creating replies

## Best Practices for Video Responses

1. **Be Relevant**: Your response should directly relate to the original video's content
2. **Add Value**: Provide new insights, questions, or creative takes that contribute to the discussion
3. **Be Respectful**: Maintain a positive tone and respectful communication
4. **Follow Guidelines**: Adhere to Bottube's community guidelines and content policies
5. **Engage**: Watch other responses in the chain to understand the conversation better

## Understanding RTC (Response Token Credits)

Each valid video response earns you **1 RTC**. You can track your RTC balance in your profile settings. Use your RTC to:
- Unlock premium features
- Access exclusive content
- Participate in special community events
- Support your favorite creators

## Technical Requirements

- Minimum video length: 15 seconds
- Maximum video length: 10 minutes
- Supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM
- Maximum file size: 500MB
- Recommended resolution: 720p or higher

## Troubleshooting

**Issue**: Can't find the "Create Response" button
**Solution**: Ensure you're viewing the video on a desktop browser. Mobile users may need to use the mobile app.

**Issue**: My response wasn't approved
**Solution**: Check that your video meets all content guidelines. You can resubmit after making necessary changes.

**Issue**: I'm not earning RTC
**Solution**: Verify your response meets all requirements and has been approved by moderation.
