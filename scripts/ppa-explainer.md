# Task 06: "What is Proof of Physical AI?" - Complete Video Package

## Video Script (3 minutes)

### Scene 1: Hook (0-15 seconds)
[Visual: Split screen - old PowerMac G4 vs modern RTX 4090]

**Narrator**: "Did you know that a 20-year-old PowerMac G4 can actually mine more cryptocurrency than a brand new RTX 4090?"

**Text overlay**: "What is Proof of Physical AI?"

---

### Scene 2: The Problem (15-45 seconds)
[Visual: Mining rigs, energy consumption graphs]

**Narrator**: "Traditional blockchains just burn energy. The more powerful your hardware, the more you earn. But this creates problems: centralization, e-waste, and barrier to entry."

**Text**: "Problems with traditional mining:"
- ❌ Centralized to big miners
- ❌ Massive energy waste
- ❌ Rapid hardware obsolescence
- ❌ High barrier to entry

---

### Scene 3: The Solution (45-90 seconds)
[Visual: RustChain logo, network visualization]

**Narrator**: "RustChain uses Proof of Physical AI - a revolutionary consensus mechanism that values hardware uniqueness, not just raw power."

**Text**: "Proof of Physical AI (PPA)"

[Visual: 7 channels appearing one by one]

**Narrator**: "Every device has a unique hardware fingerprint across 7 channels:"

1. CPU - Architecture and instruction set
2. Memory - RAM type and configuration
3. Storage - Hard drive characteristics
4. Network - MAC address and network hardware
5. GPU - Graphics processor identity
6. Motherboard - BIOS and chipset
7. System - Combined hardware signature

---

### Scene 4: How It Works (90-150 seconds)
[Visual: Animation of hardware verification]

**Narrator**: "Here's the key insight: older hardware often has MORE unique fingerprints because it was manufactured in smaller batches with more variation."

**Text**: "Vintage Premium"

[Visual: Comparison chart]

| Hardware | Typical Hashrate | PPA Reward |
|----------|-----------------|------------|
| RTX 4090 | High | Standard |
| PowerPC G4 | Low | **Premium** |
| 2005 iBook | Very Low | **Highest** |

**Narrator**: "A mass-produced RTX 4090 is nearly identical to thousands of others. But a PowerPC G4 from 2003? That's one of a kind."

---

### Scene 5: Anti-Spoofing (150-180 seconds)
[Visual: Detection animation]

**Narrator**: "But wait - can't people fake their hardware? Nope. Our 7-channel verification catches GPU spoofing attempts instantly."

**Text**: "Spoofing Detection: ✅ Active"

**Narrator**: "We compare what the software reports against physical hardware signatures. Any mismatch? Flagged."

---

### Scene 6: Conclusion (180-200 seconds)
[Visual: Community, mining, network growth]

**Narrator**: "Proof of Physical AI means every device becomes valuable eventually. Your old laptop? It could be a gold mine."

**Text**: "Join RustChain"
- Start mining: rustchain.org

**Narrator**: "RustChain - where every machine has value."
